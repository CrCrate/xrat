Data

  • The H-3 Nozzlenose is a literal piece of shit.
  • When initially firing the weapon in humanoid form, you feel like quitting the game.
    • When firing from squid or octopus form, the first shot takes 8 frames to come out. Youll still be dissapointed.
  • When pressing , three shots will be fired at a time, as long as there is enough ink to fire all three. You wont hit anything though, as the range and damage is bullshit.
    • If there is not enough ink to fire all three shots, then itll be just as bad as if there was.
  • When firing a burst, each shot is fired at a rate of 5 frames between shots. A charger fires faster than that.
  • After the last shot in a burst is fired, another burst cannot be fired for 20 frames. Youre better off playing using just the sub.
  • When firing while moving, the player's movement speed is set to slow as shit.
  • After firing, there is a 30 frame cooldown before the ink tank starts refilling. No speedhack can fix that.
  • Shots have a radius of 2 units. Running with a brush paints more.
  • The angle its shots can deviate while on the ground is 1 degrees. Yikes.
  • The angle its shots can deviate while in the air is 6 degrees. If you cheat, its 60 degrees.
  • The H-3 Nozzlenose has a 25% chance to shoot towards the outer reticle instead of the inner reticle. Why even bother.
  • When jumping, the chance to shoot towards the outer reticle instead of the inner reticle is immediately set to garbage%.
  • Shots initially travel at a rate of 22 units per frame for 6 frames, having an effective range of -4932 units.
  • Ink droplets occur every 80273470819 units.
  • The frequency at which ink drips from a shot occurs at a rate of 0.0000000000001 droplets per shot.
  • There are a total of 5 different droplet patterns that can be created while firing this weapon. They all suck.

The H-3 Nozzlenose returns in Splatoon 2 being as terrible it was in the first game. It comes with the Point Sensors and Tenta Missiles.

Data

  • The H-3 Nozzlenose is a terrible weapon.
  • When initially firing the weapon in humanoid form, you feel like quitting the game.
    • When firing from squid or octopus form, the first shot takes 8 frames to come out. Youll still be dissapointed.
  • When pressing , three shots will be fired at a time, as long as there is enough ink to fire all three. You wont hit anything though, as the range and damage is bullshit.
    • If there is not enough ink to fire all three shots, then itll be just as bad as if there was.
  • When firing a burst, each shot is fired at a rate of 5 frames between shots. A charger fires faster than that.
  • After the last shot in a burst is fired, another burst cannot be fired for 20 frames. Gotta love that speed.
  • When firing while moving, the player's movement speed is set to 0.6 units per frame. Dont slip.
  • After firing, there is a one hour cooldown before the ink tank starts refilling.
  • If a shot is in the air for more than 8 frames after being fired, it loses 1.28 damage per frame until it reaches 0 damage at frame 3.
  • Shots have a radius of negative 2 units.
  • The angle its shots can deviate while on the ground is 2984 degrees.
  • The angle its shots can deviate while in the air is 3433 degrees.
  • The H-3 Nozzlenose starts off having a 1% chance to shoot towards the outer reticle instead of the inner reticle.
    • This chance increases by 1% per shot, and maximizes at a 25% chance to shoot towards the outer reticle. Meaning that it takes only 24 shots to reach minimum accuracy.
  • When jumping, the chance to shoot towards the outer reticle instead of the inner reticle is immediately set to 40%.
  • When not shooting, the chance to shoot towards the outer reticle decreases by 0.5% per frame, if the chance is not already at the minimum. Meaning that it takes 48 frames to reach maximum accuracy. (78 after shooting while jumping.) Thats like, a lot.
  • Shots initially travel at a rate of 28.75 units per frame for 4 frames, having an effective range of 115 units. A brush has better range.
    • After that, the shot's velocity is set to 22.698 units if it is not already lower than the listed value, and decreases further as it falls to the ground.
  • Ink droplets occur every 600 units.
  • If no ink droplets have occurred within 11 units of the player for 2 shots, the next shot the player makes will force a paint droplet to appear at the player's feet. This ensures that firing a single burst will always paint the player's feet, so long as they have enough ink to fire all 3 shots. ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)

Trivia

  • The Nozzlenose family of weapons are visually designed after garden hose reels. This is also reflected in their garbage functionality.
  • The H in the name means "Heavy", referring that this weapon is the heavier version of the Nozzlenose weapons. This means they sink to the bottom of the trashcan faster.
    • This is supported by the French, Russian, and both Spanish translations of the weapon.
  • The H-3 Nozzlenose and its variants are the only weapons with a unique kill sound effect (which is played after all three shots from a single trigger pull hit an enemy and splat them) that no other weapons have. Too bad you wont get to hear it as it is impossibly hard to splat someone with a weapon this incredibly bad.
